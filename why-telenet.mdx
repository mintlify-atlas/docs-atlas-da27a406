## Real Speed, Real Performance

### What Makes Our Fiber Different

Unlike traditional ADSL or cable internet, Telenet's fiber optic network transmits data using light instead of electrical signals. This means:

- **Higher speeds**: Up to 1000 Mbps on our Pro Gaming plan
- **Lower latency**: Perfect for gaming, video calls, and real-time applications
- **Greater stability**: No slowdowns during peak hours
- **Symmetrical bandwidth**: Upload and download at the same speed

<Note>
All Telenet plans feature symmetrical fiber optic connections (↑↓), meaning your upload speed equals your download speed—essential for video conferencing, cloud backups, and content creation.
</Note>
