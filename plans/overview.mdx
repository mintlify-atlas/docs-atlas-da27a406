## Choosing the Right Plan

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How much speed do I need?">
    - **300 Mbps**: 1-3 people, browsing, streaming, video calls
    - **600 Mbps**: 3-5 people, multiple devices, 4K streaming, remote work
    - **1000 Mbps**: 5+ people, gaming, large file transfers, smart home devices
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What are symmetric speeds?">
    Symmetric speeds mean your upload speed equals your download speed. Most ISPs offer asymmetric connections (like 300 Mbps download / 20 Mbps upload). With Telenet's symmetric fiber, a 300 Mbps plan gives you 300 Mbps in BOTH directions. This is crucial for:
    
    - Video conferencing
    - Cloud backups
    - Live streaming
    - Uploading large files
    - Online gaming
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade later?">
    Yes! All our plans are flexible. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time without penalties or contracts.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between WiFi 6 and WiFi 6E?">
    WiFi 6E (included with Pro Gaming) is the latest generation that adds support for the 6 GHz band, providing:
    
    - Less interference from neighboring networks
    - Lower latency for gaming and real-time applications
    - More capacity for multiple devices
    - Faster speeds on compatible devices
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
