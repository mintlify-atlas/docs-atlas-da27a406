## WiFi 6E: The Next Generation

WiFi 6E provides significant advantages over WiFi 6:

### 6 GHz Band Benefits
- **Less interference** - New frequency band with minimal congestion
- **Lower latency** - Crucial for gaming and real-time applications
- **More channels** - 7 additional 160 MHz channels
- **Faster speeds** - Up to 30% faster than WiFi 6
- **More capacity** - Better performance with many devices

### Gaming-Specific Features
- **Reduced jitter** - More consistent frame times
- **Better QoS** - Traffic prioritization for gaming packets
- **Faster handoff** - Seamless roaming between access points
- **WPA3 security** - Latest encryption without performance impact

<Note>
  To take full advantage of WiFi 6E, your devices need WiFi 6E support. However, the router is backward compatible with all WiFi 5 and WiFi 6 devices, which will still benefit from the improved routing and mesh system.
</Note>
