## Making Your Decision

### Choose Básico if:
- You're price-sensitive and need basic reliable internet
- You have 1-3 light to moderate users
- Your home is under 1,500 sq ft
- You don't need mesh coverage
- You want to try fiber internet at low cost

### Choose Hogar+ if:
- You want the best overall value (most popular choice)
- You have 3-5 users with varied needs
- You need whole-home WiFi coverage

- You want room to grow without overpaying

### Choose Pro Gaming if:
- You're a competitive gamer or streamer
- You need a fixed IP address
- You have 5+ heavy users
- You want the absolute best performance
- You have a large home or property
- Budget is less of a concern than performance
