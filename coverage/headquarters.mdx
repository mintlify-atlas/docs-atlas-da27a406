## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Do I need an appointment to visit?">
  No appointment is necessary for most services. However, during peak hours (lunch time and early evening), you may experience a short wait. For complex technical issues or business consultations, calling ahead or messaging on WhatsApp can help ensure a team member is available.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I pay my bill in person?">
  Yes! We accept in-person payments at our headquarters during business hours. Bring your account number for faster processing.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I arrive outside business hours?">
  Our physical office is only staffed during posted business hours. For urgent support outside these hours, please contact us via WhatsApp or check our support documentation online.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is customer parking available?">
  Street parking is available in the area surrounding Edificio Telenet. Our ground-floor location makes access convenient once you've parked.
</Accordion>
