## Professional Installation Included

Every Telenet fiber optic internet service includes **free professional installation** by our certified technicians. We handle everything from running the fiber optic cable to configuring your network.

<Info>
  **Installation is completely free** - no hidden fees or charges. This includes all necessary equipment, cable installation, and network setup.
</Info>

## Installation Timeline

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up & Schedule">
    After signing up for service (in person, via WhatsApp, or online), our team will:
    - Confirm coverage at your address
    - Schedule an installation appointment within 48-72 hours
    - Provide a specific date and time window
    
    **Typical scheduling:** 2-3 business days from sign-up
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Pre-Installation Preparation">
    Before our technicians arrive, you'll receive:
    - Confirmation call or WhatsApp message 24 hours before
    - Technician contact information
    - List of items to prepare (see below)
    
    **Duration:** 1 day before installation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Installation Day">
    Our certified technicians will:
    - Install fiber optic cable from the street to your location
    - Set up the ONT (Optical Network Terminal)
    - Configure your WiFi router with custom network name and password
    - Test connection speed and stability
    - Show you how to use the equipment
    
    **Duration:** 2-4 hours depending on your property
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Service Activation">
    Your internet service is activated immediately upon installation completion:
    - Connect your devices to WiFi
    - Enjoy high-speed fiber optic internet
    - Receive technician contact info for any follow-up questions
    
    **You're online!**
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to Prepare for Installation

### Before the Technician Arrives

<Note>
  **Essential Preparations:**
  
  1. **Clear Access Paths** - Ensure technicians can access:
     - Exterior walls where cable will enter
     - Interior rooms where equipment will be installed
     - Path from street to building
  
  2. **Decide Router Location** - Choose where you want your WiFi router:
     - Central location in your home/business for best coverage
     - Near a power outlet
     - Accessible but secure location
  
  3. **Be Present** - Someone 18+ must be present during the entire installation
  
  4. **Secure Pets** - Keep pets in a separate area during installation
  
  5. **Notify Building Management** - If you live in an apartment or gated community, inform management about the scheduled installation
</Note>

## Installation Methods

Our technicians use professional-grade installation techniques:

### Fiber Cable Installation

<Accordion title="Aerial Installation">
  For properties with existing utility poles, we typically run fiber optic cable aerially:
  - Cable runs from utility pole to your building
  - Properly secured with weather-resistant hardware
  - Minimal visual impact
  - Fastest installation method
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Underground Installation">
  For properties with underground utilities:
  - Cable is buried in protective conduit
  - Entry point through exterior wall
  - More time-intensive but completely hidden
  - May require additional scheduling
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Interior Routing">
  Once cable enters your building:
  - Routed along baseboards or through walls (with permission)
  - Professional cable management
  - Clean, organized appearance
  - Equipment placement in your chosen location
</Accordion>

## Equipment Setup

### What Gets Installed

<Steps>
  <Step title="ONT (Optical Network Terminal)">
    The ONT is installed where the fiber optic cable enters your property:
    - Converts optical signal to ethernet
    - Usually wall-mounted
    - Requires power outlet
    - Always-on device (do not unplug)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="WiFi Router">
    Connected to the ONT, typically in a central location:
    - Provides WiFi throughout your space
    - Configured with your custom network name
    - Secure password set by technician
    - Multiple device connections supported
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Network Configuration">
    Technician will:
    - Set up your WiFi network name (SSID)
    - Create a secure password
    - Connect a test device
    - Verify full speed delivery
    - Provide you with WiFi credentials
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Installation Day Experience

### What to Expect

**Arrival:** Technicians arrive in marked Telenet vehicles during your scheduled window

**Site Survey:** Brief assessment of optimal installation route (5-10 minutes)

**Installation Work:** Cable running and equipment setup (1.5-3 hours)

**Testing:** Speed tests and connection verification (15-30 minutes)

**Walkthrough:** Technician shows you how to use your service (10-15 minutes)

<Info>
  **Total Time:** Most installations complete in 2-4 hours. Complex installations (multi-story buildings, long cable runs) may take longer.
</Info>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="How long does installation take?">
  Most installations complete in 2-4 hours. Factors that affect timing include:
  - Distance from street connection point
  - Property type (single home vs. apartment)
  - Installation method (aerial vs. underground)
  - Number of floors or rooms involved
  
  Our technicians will give you a better estimate when they arrive and assess your property.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Will installation damage my property?">
  Our technicians are trained to minimize any impact:
  - Small exterior penetrations are sealed and weatherproofed
  - Interior cable routing uses non-destructive methods
  - Any necessary holes are professionally finished
  - We clean up all installation debris
  
  For renters: We recommend getting landlord approval before installation.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I choose where the router goes?">
  Yes! Within reason, you can choose your router location. We recommend:
  - Central location in your home/office
  - Away from major obstacles (thick walls, metal)
  - Elevated position (shelf or mounted)
  - Near where you use internet most
  
  Our technicians will advise on optimal placement for best WiFi coverage.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I need to reschedule?">
  Contact us via WhatsApp or call our headquarters at least 24 hours before your appointment:
  - We'll reschedule at no charge
  - New appointment typically within 2-3 days
  - No penalties for rescheduling
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is installation really free?">
  Yes, completely free! There are no installation fees, equipment rental fees, or hidden charges. This is included with your service subscription.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I have issues after installation?">
  Contact us immediately via:
  - WhatsApp for quick support
  - Visit our headquarters during business hours
  - Call the technician contact number provided
  
  We provide follow-up support to ensure your service is working perfectly.
</Accordion>
