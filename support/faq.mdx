## Common Questions

Get instant answers to the most frequently asked questions about our fiber optic internet service.

<Note>
Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us via [WhatsApp](/support/contact) and we'll respond instantly.
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is fiber optic and what advantages does it have?">
    Fiber optics transmit data through light instead of electrical signals, which allows for much higher speeds, lower latency, and greater stability than traditional ADSL or coaxial cable.

    **Key benefits:**
    - Ultra-fast speeds up to 1000 Mbps
    - Low latency for gaming and video calls
    - More stable and reliable connection
    - Future-proof technology
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does installation take?">
    In less than 48 business hours from signing up, our technical team will complete the installation at your home, at no additional cost.

    **What's included:**
    - Professional installation by certified technicians
    - Router configuration and setup
    - Connection testing and optimization
    - Zero installation fees
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a data limit or download quota?">
    No. All our plans come with unlimited data. You can browse, stream in 4K, and work remotely without worrying about limits.

    **Truly unlimited:**
    - No data caps
    - No throttling
    - No fair usage policy
    - Stream, game, and download as much as you want
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change plans after signing up?">
    Of course. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time without penalty. The change is applied from the next billing cycle.

    **Flexible plans:**
    - Upgrade anytime to get faster speeds
    - Downgrade if your needs change
    - No penalties or fees for changing plans
    - Changes take effect next billing cycle
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you include the WiFi router?">
    Yes, all plans include a state-of-the-art router. The Pro plan includes WiFi 6 and the Ultra includes WiFi 6E with extended coverage.

    **Router specifications by plan:**
    - **Básico:** WiFi 6 router included
    - **Hogar+:** WiFi 6 router + 1 Mesh repeater
    - **Pro Gaming:** WiFi 6E router + 2 Mesh repeaters + Fixed IP
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a minimum contract period?">
    There is no minimum commitment. You can cancel the service whenever you want without early termination fees.

    **No lock-in:**
    - Cancel anytime
    - No early termination fees
    - No questions asked
    - Keep your router (after 12 months)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What speeds can I expect in real-world use?">
    Our fiber optic connections are symmetrical, meaning you get the same upload and download speeds. You should expect:
    - **Básico (300 Mbps):** Perfect for browsing, streaming HD, and video calls
    - **Hogar+ (600 Mbps):** Ideal for multiple devices, 4K streaming, and remote work
    - **Pro Gaming (1000 Mbps):** Ultimate speed for gaming, streaming, and heavy downloads
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many devices can I connect?">
    There's no limit to the number of devices you can connect. Our routers support multiple simultaneous connections without speed degradation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer static IP addresses?">
    Yes, static IP addresses are included with the Pro Gaming plan. If you're on a different plan and need a static IP, contact our support team for options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is IPv6 supported?">
    Yes, all our plans support IPv6, ensuring future compatibility and better performance for modern applications.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
