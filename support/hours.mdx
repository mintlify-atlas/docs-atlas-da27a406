## Emergency Support

### Critical Issues

For service outages or critical technical problems:

1. **Contact immediately via WhatsApp or phone** (24/7)
2. Our team will diagnose the issue remotely
3. If needed, a technician will be dispatched
4. VIP customers receive priority scheduling

<Warning>
**Complete service outage?** Call or WhatsApp us immediately. Our emergency response team is available 24/7 to restore your service as quickly as possible.
</Warning>
