## Verify Service Availability

Before signing up for Telenet fiber optic internet, confirm that our service is available at your location. Our fiber optic network is expanding throughout Barinas, Venezuela.

<Info>
Telenet currently serves the Barinas metropolitan area with plans to expand coverage to surrounding regions.
</Info>

## How to Check Coverage

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Our Office">
    Come to our main office at Local 2-97, Sector Independencia I during business hours. Our team can instantly verify coverage for your address.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Provide Your Address">
    Share your complete address with our customer service representative. We'll check our fiber optic network map to confirm availability.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Get Confirmation">
    Receive immediate confirmation of service availability and estimated installation timeline (typically within 48 hours).
  </Step>
</Steps>
