## What to Expect on Installation Day

### Before the Technician Arrives

<Note>
Prepare your home for installation by clearing access to where you want the router located and ensuring someone 18+ will be present during the appointment.
</Note>

**Preparation Checklist:**

- Clear the area where you want the router installed
- Ensure access to exterior walls if fiber needs to be run into the home
- Have a power outlet available near the desired router location
- Be ready to show the technician around your home to determine the best cable routing

### During Installation

The installation process includes:

1. **Site Survey** - The technician inspects your property to determine the best route for the fiber optic cable
2. **Fiber Cable Installation** - Running the fiber optic line from the street connection point to your home
3. **ONT Setup** - Installing and connecting the Optical Network Terminal
4. **Router Configuration** - Setting up your WiFi router with your custom network name and password
5. **Mesh Setup** (Hogar+ and Pro Gaming only) - Positioning and configuring Mesh repeaters for optimal coverage
6. **Speed Testing** - Verifying you're receiving your plan's full speed
7. **Walkthrough** - Showing you how to connect devices and manage your network

<Warning>
The installation may require drilling small holes to route cables. Our technicians will discuss placement with you before making any modifications to your property.
</Warning>

### Installation Timeline

<Accordion title="How long does installation take?">
  Most installations are completed in 2-4 hours. Complex installations (multi-story homes, long cable runs) may take up to 6 hours. Your technician will provide an estimated completion time upon arrival.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need to be home the entire time?">
  Yes, an adult (18+) must be present during the entire installation. The technician will need access to various areas of your home and requires approval for cable routing decisions.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if installation can't be completed the same day?">
  In rare cases where unforeseen obstacles arise, we'll reschedule to complete the installation. You won't be charged until service is active.
</Accordion>

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

<Accordion title="My internet isn't working after installation">
  1. Check that the ONT has a solid green light (not blinking)
  2. Verify the router is powered on and all cables are securely connected
  3. Restart your router by unplugging it for 30 seconds
  4. If issues persist, contact our 24/7 support team
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="WiFi signal is weak in some rooms">
  If you have the Hogar+ or Pro Gaming plan, ensure your Mesh repeaters are properly positioned. They should be placed between the router and areas with weak signal, not at the edge of coverage. Contact support for a free coverage assessment.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I'm not getting my full speed">
  - Test speed using an ethernet cable connection (WiFi may be slower)
  - Close all applications and pause downloads/uploads during the test
  - Ensure no other devices are using the connection during testing
  - Older devices may not support your plan's full speed
  
  If speeds are still low, contact support for a line check.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I move the router to a different room?">
  The router location is determined during installation based on where the fiber enters your home. Moving it may require relocating the ONT. Contact our technical team to discuss relocation options.
</Accordion>
