## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="What does symmetric speed mean?">
  Symmetric speed means your upload and download speeds are identical. For example, the 600 Mbps Hogar+ plan gives you 600 Mbps for downloads AND 600 Mbps for uploads. This is a major advantage of fiber optic over cable internet, making it perfect for video calls, cloud backups, and content creation.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?">
  Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Contact our support team, and we'll adjust your service. Plan changes typically take effect within 24 hours.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are there any data caps or throttling?">
  No! All Telenet plans include unlimited data with no caps, throttling, or slowdowns. You get your full speed 24/7.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What's the difference between WiFi 6 and WiFi 6E?">
  WiFi 6E (included with Pro Gaming) adds support for the 6 GHz frequency band, providing even faster speeds and less interference in crowded areas. WiFi 6 (included with Básico and Hogar+) is already excellent for most households.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need the Mesh repeaters?">
  Mesh repeaters extend your WiFi coverage to eliminate dead zones in larger homes or homes with thick walls. If you have a small apartment, you may not need them. Larger homes benefit greatly from the extended coverage.
</Accordion>
