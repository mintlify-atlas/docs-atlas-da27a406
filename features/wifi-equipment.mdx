## WiFi 6 Technology

<Info>
  WiFi 6 (802.11ax) is the latest WiFi standard, offering up to **4x better performance** than WiFi 5 in congested environments.
</Info>

### Key WiFi 6 Benefits

**Faster Speeds**
- Up to 9.6 Gbps theoretical maximum (vs. 3.5 Gbps on WiFi 5)
- Real-world speeds of 300-1000 Mbps on compatible devices

**Better Device Handling**
- OFDMA technology divides channels for multiple devices simultaneously
- Connect 50+ devices without performance degradation
- Ideal for smart homes with many IoT devices

**Improved Range**
- Enhanced signal strength through beamforming
- Better wall penetration for multi-floor homes
- Reduced dead zones compared to older standards

**Battery Efficiency**
- Target Wake Time (TWT) reduces power consumption on mobile devices
- Smartphones and tablets last longer on WiFi 6

<Tip>
  All Telenet routers support both WiFi 6 and older standards (WiFi 5, WiFi 4), so your existing devices will work perfectly while newer devices get the full WiFi 6 benefits.
</Tip>

## WiFi 6E: The Pro Gaming Advantage

The **Pro Gaming plan** includes a WiFi 6E router, which adds access to the newly opened **6 GHz frequency band**.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is the 6 GHz Band?">
    WiFi 6E extends WiFi 6 into the 6 GHz spectrum, providing:
    
    - **1200 MHz of additional bandwidth** (more than 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz combined)
    - **7 additional 160 MHz channels** for ultra-fast connections
    - **Zero interference** from older devices that don't support 6 GHz
    
    This means your gaming PC, VR headset, or streaming devices can connect on a completely uncongested frequency.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="6 GHz Benefits for Gaming">
    - **Lower latency**: Less channel congestion = faster response times
    - **Consistent speeds**: No interference from neighbors' WiFi networks
    - **Multi-gig WiFi**: Capable of delivering the full 1000 Mbps wirelessly
    - **Future-ready**: Support for emerging WiFi 7 devices
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Device Compatibility">
    WiFi 6E requires compatible devices:
    
    - Recent gaming laptops (2022+)
    - High-end smartphones (Samsung S21 Ultra+, iPhone 15 Pro)
    - PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S
    - Meta Quest 3 VR headset
    
    Older devices automatically connect to 5 GHz or 2.4 GHz bands instead.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Mesh Repeater System

<Note>
  Mesh repeaters extend your WiFi coverage throughout your entire home, eliminating dead zones and ensuring consistent speeds in every room.
</Note>

### How Mesh Technology Works

Unlike traditional WiFi extenders, mesh repeaters:

- **Use the same network name (SSID)**: Devices seamlessly roam between router and repeaters
- **Smart routing**: Automatically connect devices to the strongest signal point
- **Backhaul optimization**: Communicate with the main router efficiently to minimize speed loss

### Coverage by Plan

| Plan | Equipment | Recommended Coverage |
|------|-----------|---------------------|
| Básico | WiFi 6 Router only | Up to 1,500 sq ft / 140 m² |
| Hogar+ | WiFi 6 Router + 1 Mesh | Up to 3,000 sq ft / 280 m² |
| Pro Gaming | WiFi 6E Router + 2 Mesh | Up to 4,500 sq ft / 420 m² |

<Tip>
  Place mesh repeaters in central locations between the main router and areas with weak signal. Avoid placing them inside cabinets or behind large metal objects.
</Tip>
